Lab 2 Writeup - Trina Goetz

My design decisions for this lab are as follows: 
    I chose not to implement a header page or free-space map, and instead just get the number of pages from the file size. This kept 
        the heap file simple and avoided maintaining extra metadata.
    For insertion, I used a linear scan of pages to find available space rather than tracking free space across pages. This was simpler
        than keeping the map of free space and fit the specs for this lab.
    I implemented update as delete + insert to avoid handling in-place resizing of values, since values can change size.
    In the iterator, I used the existing page iterator (IntoIterator for Page) rather than reimplementing slot traversal logic, but
        I added a helper to allow the iterator to start from a given slot (new_from), since the provided page iterator only started 
        at slot 0.

I spent around 4 hours on this lab, including planning/designing and implementing the functions required. The design was clear overall, 
    but the insertion logic and iterator edge cases were the most challenging parts for me to figure out.
